Hello world!

Lo-Ellen Robotics, designated as team 4069, is a robotics team from Lo-Ellen Park Secondary School in Sudbury, Ontario. We're a new team to the FIRST competition. As the only robotics team in a Sudbury high school, it will be an engaging challenge to compete on equal footing with more experienced teams from all over Ontario.

FRC

FRC, or FIRST Robotics Competition, is the highest level of robotics competition offered by FIRST. It is also a high school competition, which means that high school students team up to build 5 foot tall (150 cm) robots.

About Us

The Lo-Ellen Robotics team is based out of LoEllen Park Secondary School of Sudbury, Ontario. The team is comprised of a diverse group of students from grade 9 to grade 12.
